About the practice

A family practice built on trust and long-standing patient relationships

G K Ooi & Associates is a family-run dental practice in the heart of Rotherhithe, caring for patients across South London since 1998. Over the years we have grown into a close team while keeping the personal, familiar service our patients know us for — many have been with us for decades.

We offer both NHS and private treatment, so whether you need a routine check-up or more specialised private care, we can help. Our dentists, hygienists and nurses take the time to listen, explain everything clearly, and work at a pace that suits you.

We treat patients of all ages and take particular care with those who feel nervous or anxious about visiting the dentist. Your comfort, and your trust, matter to us.

Our services

A variety of available treatments

    Private: from £55 (consultation)

    NHS: Band 1 — £26.80

  • A comprehensive examination of your teeth, gums and mouth, including X-rays where needed. We review your dental history and flag anything that needs attention early. Regular check-ups are the best way to avoid bigger problems later.

    Private: £55–£80

    NHS: Band 1 — £26.80

  • Restorative treatments to repair or replace damaged, decayed or missing teeth. Fillings restore a single tooth, while crowns and bridges rebuild strength and fill gaps for a natural look and feel. We'll talk you through the best option for your situation.

    Private: Fillings from £120 · Crowns from £600 · Bridges from £700 per unit

    NHS: Fillings — Band 2 — £73.50 · Crowns & bridges — Band 3 — £319.10

  • Treatment for gum inflammation and periodontal disease, which if left untreated can lead to tooth loss. We clean below the gumline and give you a plan to get your gums healthy again. Early treatment makes a real difference.

    Private: from £100

    NHS: Band 2 — £73.50

  • A way to save a tooth affected by infection or deep decay, rather than removing it. We carefully clean and seal the inside of the tooth to relieve pain and protect it for the future. Modern techniques make the procedure comfortable.

    Private: from £450

    NHS: Band 2 — £73.50

  • A professional hygienist clean to remove plaque and tartar that brushing can't reach. It leaves your teeth feeling fresh and helps keep your gums healthy. A great routine treatment alongside your regular check-ups.
